About

Hello, I'm Hana Gharrad, a passionate data scientist and software engineer with a deep interest in urban mobility and smart cities. My research and work span various domains, including data analysis, machine learning, IoT, Multi-agent Systems, Mobility Simulation and transportation systems.

Currently I'm researcher at the Transportation Research Institute (IMOB) UHasselt Belgium working on my doctoral thesis in drone’s collaboration for smart cities applications. I'm a member of Transportation Behavior research group. I was a reseach assistant at German University of Technology in Oman. I hold a master degree in Statistics and Data Scince from Data Science Institure (DSI), UHasselt , Belgium and a software engineer degree from Higher Institute of Applied Sciences and Technologies, Tunisia. My research interest includes Data Analsyis, Machine learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI) Multi-Agent Systems, Self-Organization and Drone Collaboration, Transportation, IoT and Smart Cities.
